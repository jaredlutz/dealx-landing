We are launching this insights hub as a quiet counterpart to the rest of the site: fewer slogans, more context. You will find **short essays**, process notes, and reminders about how to read offering materials—not hot takes on markets.

## Who this is for

- Accredited and institutional investors evaluating private credit and related sleeves  
- Advisors who need clear language to share with investment committees  
- Anyone who prefers footnotes to hype  

## What this is not

Nothing here is personalized investment advice, tax advice, or a recommendation to buy or sell securities. Our programs are described in their **official offering documents** and are available only where lawfully offered.
